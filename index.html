About

How the Two founders Created the Business from scratch.

  • 2015-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Looking to take theyâ€™re first steps into the work force, our founders found themselves wondering what they needed to do in order to get their career started. After the consultation of guidance counselors, they came to the consensus that they needed the help of professionals, those who understand what employers are seeking, to help them build an exceptional resume that would increase their chances of landing that job. However, after an extensive search, the founders were astonished at the lack of services provided. There was no company present within any market that offered resume building services to up and coming youths who desired to join the workforce. It was at this point in time that they realized that this is not only a plausible business idea but an extraordinary opportunity for them to step into an untapped market. Not only would it be a great means of capital, but it would also allow the founders to help others that found themselves in the same shoes.

  • January 2016

    An Agency is Born

    The Founder's quickly learned how to make an effective and efficent online resume/portfolio by asking their gudiance Counsler and family for tips. they Named the company Nobu digital and started to Market their company to students across Waterloo that need help on making and effective online resume/portoflio.

  • March 2016

    Transition to Full Service

    After 2 months Nobu Digital skyrocketded to one of Waterloo's top resorces that students used to improve thier online presence.

  • September 2016

    Phase Two Expansion

    Currently Nobu digital is trying to expand there market to other univeristy that have students that want to improve their online resume/portfolio
